Dark/Light mode switching

In the click of a button, SchoolTools can automatically switch from light and dark mode on your PC. It can also set the default wallpapers for their respective color modes.

Custom Walpapers

SchoolTools bypasses all school or work set restrictions on walpapers, allowing you to express yourself with a custom walpaper.

Easy to run

Just extract, run, and enjoy. No need for useless installers.
